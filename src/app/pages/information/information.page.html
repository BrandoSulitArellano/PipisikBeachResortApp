BACKGROUND AND HISTORY

What is Pipisik

#1

Pipisik is a kind of tree living in the seashore.
It was found by Felipe V. Sulit.

Felipe V. Sulit

#2

Founder of Pipisik

Family First

#3

The land where Pipisik Beach Resort is standing today is from Felipe's father. Way back 90's, Felipe decided to build a rest house for his own family to stay there.

Legend

#4

Years later, Felipe decided to build something better. He wanted to make it a profitable place to gain extra money for his family. Through faith and perseverance, Pipisik Beach Resort is now one of the most popular resort in Hugom, San Juan Batangas, His hardwork is now a legend, a legend that changed his family's life.
